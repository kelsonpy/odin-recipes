Cranberry Orange Smoothie

Description

The Cranberry-Orange Smoothie
recipe combines fresh cranberries, ripe bananas, and orange juice for a refreshing and vibrant drink. Simply blend together the cranberries and bananas with yogurt, orange juice, and a touch of honey until smooth.

This nutrient-packed smoothie is not only delicious but also rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, making it a perfect healthy treat to start your day or enjoy as a snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs flaxseeds
  • 1 tbsp hemp seeds
  • 1 banana
  • 1 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/2 scoop
  • 1/2 cup frozen cranberries
  • 1 tsp bee pollen
  • honey to taste

Steps

  1. Blend
  2. Pour
