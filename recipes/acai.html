Acai Bowl

Description

This Homemade Acai Bowl recipe uses just 3 simple vegan ingredients for the most delicious DIY açai smoothie bowl ever! Using a high-powered blender, it's ready within 5 minutes. Customize with your favorite toppings, like fresh berries, coconut flakes, and granola.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet unsweetened frozen acai berry blend.
  • 1 banana, frozen
  • ¼ to ½ cup non-dairy milk or yogurt
  • Fresh berries, granola, peanut butter

Steps

  1. In a high-powered blender, combine the acai berry packet, frozen banana, and ¼ cup dairy-free milk of choice.
  2. Blend until completely smooth, adding more dairy-free milk if necessary until your smoothie will blend smoothly.
  3. Pour into a bowl and top with your favorite toppings.
